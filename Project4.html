PROJECT 4

IMERSO-UNDERWATER HOTEL

My project is based on an experience in an underwater hotel for a certain number of days, along the Portuguese coast. This way, when the water levels rise, we will be able to see all of our heritage that ended up submerged. We can see our species without them needing to be removed from their natural habitat and transported to aquariums. Consequently, we would enjoy and value the Portuguese coast more since the hotel's itinerary contains stops at 7 national points.
